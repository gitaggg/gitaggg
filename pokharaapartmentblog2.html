Discover why staying in a lakeside apartment in Pokhara can make your trip unforgettable. Enjoy stunning views, privacy, and the freedom of apartment living.

Why Choose Lakeside for Your Stay in Pokhara?

The Lakeside area of Pokhara is one of the most vibrant and picturesque parts of the city. Overlooking Phewa Lake and framed by the Annapurna mountain range, Lakeside offers a unique blend of nature, culture, and relaxation. Staying in a lakeside apartment provides easy access to the best attractions while ensuring a peaceful retreat.

Advantages of Staying in a Lakeside Apartment

  • Scenic Views: Wake up to the beautiful sight of Phewa Lake and enjoy breathtaking sunsets from the comfort of your apartment.
  • Close to Attractions: Most lakeside apartments are within walking distance of popular Pokhara landmarks, cafes, and shopping spots, making it easy to explore the city.
  • Private and Relaxing: Apartments offer a quieter, more private alternative to hotels, perfect for those looking to unwind.
  • Freedom to Cook: With fully equipped kitchens, lakeside apartments let you enjoy home-cooked meals with locally-sourced ingredients, adding a unique touch to your travel experience.

Top Things to Do Around Lakeside, Pokhara

From adventure sports to cultural sites, Lakeside is at the heart of Pokhara’s attractions. Here are some activities to consider:

  • Boat Rides on Phewa Lake: Rent a boat or take a tour on the lake to enjoy the serene beauty and reach the famous Tal Barahi Temple.
  • Hiking and Trekking: Lakeside is a starting point for several scenic trails, including hikes to Sarangkot for sunrise views over the Himalayas.
  • Shopping and Dining: Explore Lakeside’s vibrant streets lined with unique shops, traditional markets, and restaurants serving Nepali and international cuisine.
